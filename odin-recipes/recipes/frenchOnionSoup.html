French Onion Soup

Description

This full-flavored soup gets its rich flavor from caramelized onions (the best!), a good quality beef broth or stock, and toasted cheesy baguette slices. It’s the recipe you’ll turn to when you want a meal that feels fancy but is simple to prepare. (quote taken from here.)

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Butter and Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • White Wine
  • Beef Broth or Stock
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme and Bay Leaves
  • Baguette Slices and Gruyere Cheese

Steps

  1. Caramelize the onions.
  2. Stir in the flour and garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Deglaze the pot by pouring in the white wine and scrap browned bits from bottom of the pot.
  4. Add beef broth, salt, pepper, thyme and bay leaves.
  5. Simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes- stirring occasionally.
  6. While soup simmers, toast baguette slices on a baking sheet in the oven.
  7. Place 4 oven-safe soup bowls on a heavy rimmed baking sheet, and ladle soup into the bowls.
  8. Top each bowl with 2 toasted baguette slices, browned-side down, and shredded Gruyere cheese.
  9. Put the baking sheet in the oven and broil for 2-4 minutes.
