Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter and Cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Crispy Bacon Bits
  • Grated Cheese
  • Soured Cream
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Bake potatoes in the oven until crisp on the outside, and tender in the middle.
  2. Slice potatoes in half and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add butter, cream, salt and pepper, and mash together.
  4. Stir in crispy bacon bits and grated cheese.
  5. Spoon the mixture back into the potato skins, and place on a baking tray.
  6. Sprinkle on more cheese, then place back in oven for 10 minutes, until cheese has melted.
  7. Serve topped with a little soured cream and a sprinkling of scallions.
